GDFNow.org

Portal rewarding good Deeds!

GDFNow.org actually stands for “Good Deed Feed - Now”. It is a not-for profit social networking portal that aims to connect the positive acts (Deeds) being performed by the Individuals and Groups, and build a culture where these Deeds get rewarded by Businesses, Government Agencies and NGOs. By rewarding the Deeds and their doers (Deeders), we believe that doing deeds will turn into a mass movement and will motivate more and more people to become Deeders and contribute to make this whole world a better place to live in - for all of us - and our ecology i.e the Environment.
